Katie Coffee Cake

Preheat oven to 375F

2 1/2 Cups Flour
3/4 Cup Sugar
3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
3/4 Teaspoon Salt
1 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
1/2 Cup Oil

-----

1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
1 Cup Buttermilk
2 Eggs

=====

Mix first seven ingredients until crumbly, reserve 1/2 Cup for topping

Add last four ingredients, put in a greased 9x13 pan, sprinkle with reserved 1/2 Cup topping.

Bake for 25-30 minutes.
