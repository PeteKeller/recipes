Sweet and Sour Sauce (for egg rolls)

Ingredients
3/4 c sugar
1/2 cup catsup
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1/4 Tsp salt
2/3 cup water

1 Tbsp cornstarch
1/3 cup water

1/4 cup apple cider vinegar.

Mix sugar, catsup soy, salt and 2/3 cup water in pot and bring to a boil. while waiting for it to boil, mix cornstarch with 1/3 cup water. When it starts to boil, stir water and cornstarch together to mix and add to pot and stir in to thicken.  Stir in vinegar.
