Lombard Chicken Pasties

Serves 6

12 oz pie crust
2 eggs beaten
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1 lb chicken or turkey breast meat cut into small pieces.
6-9 strips of bacon trimmed of fat and cut into 1 inch pieces.

Mix beaten eggs with lemon juice, pepper and ginger.
Place the slices of meat in the mixture and marinade overnight.

Roll the pastry and cut into cirlces each about 4 inches across. 'rest' it while making the filling.  Pre-heat the oven to 425.

Place the chicken on one side of the round, but not right up to the edge.
Lay a piece of bacon on each pile.

brush the edge of the pastry with any remaining egg mixture, use a third egg if necessary.
fold the bare half of each pastry round over the meat and match the two pastry edges.
pinch the edges together, fluting them, or press with a fork.  Prick the pastry in several places.

Bake on a baking sheet for 10 minutes, reduce heat to 375, and cook for another 15 minutes.

serve hot or cold. 

Freezes well.
