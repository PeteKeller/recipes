Egg Roll (Mini)

Wrapper
Wonton Sheets (skins)

4 cups uncooked cabbage, finely shredded
2 cups cooked chicken, minced
1/2 cup finely shreadded onion
1/2 cup finely shreadded celery
1 tbsp corn oil
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp Chinese soy sauce

In a large deep bowl, place chicken, onion, celery, oil, salt, pepper, soy and mix until thoroughly blended.
Add cabbage, stir gently.

Roll in skins
Place a skin in front of you in a diamond pattern. Place a spoonfull of filling mixture in the center of each skin.  With a beaten egg, brush the edges of the skin, so that the edges will stick together when folded.  Fold the top point down, fold the left and right points in, ensuring the skin it tightly wrapped against the mixture.  roll the skin down onto the bottom point keeping the skin tight against the mixture.  Press firmly to ensure all openings are fully sealed.

Pre-cook the egg rolls.  Drop egg rolls into 350 Degree oil, and cook for 1 minute only.  Put on a plate and chill in the refrigerator.

When needed cook in 350 degree oil until golden.
